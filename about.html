Craftsmanship, sustainability & the perfect cup

Meticulously Sourced, Ethically Traded

We travel to the highlands of Ethiopia, Colombia, and Sumatra to bring you single-origin beans that tell a story. Each batch is small-roasted in London to unlock vibrant notes of chocolate, berries, and caramel.

From bean to cup — we believe in ethical trade, bold flavour, and the warmth of a perfect brew. We partner directly with family-owned farms, ensuring fair wages and sustainable practices.

Our Sustainability Promise

We've committed to becoming carbon neutral by 2026. Our roastery runs on renewable energy, and all packaging is 100% compostable or recyclable. For every bag sold, we plant one tree in coffee-growing regions.
